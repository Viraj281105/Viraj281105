### 📄 Research Projects

#### 1. CausoScope — Structural Causal Modeling for Policy Evaluation
*Formal causal inference framework for climate policy analysis*

- Formulated structural causal models (SCMs) linking policy interventions (carbon pricing, subsidy allocation) with measurable environmental and economic outcomes, explicitly encoding assumptions via directed acyclic graphs and structural equations
- Conducted identifiability analysis under backdoor and frontdoor criteria; implemented ATE and CATE estimators using inverse probability weighting and doubly robust methods
- Performed counterfactual simulations across 12+ policy intervention scenarios using do-calculus
- Detected instability under latent confounding and partial observability; introduced sensitivity analysis (Rosenbaum bounds, E-values) to quantify robustness bounds
- Characterized estimator variance across bootstrapped samples (n=1000)

---

#### 2. Entailment-Based Hallucination Detection in LLMs
*NLI framework for factual reliability*

- Formulated hallucination detection as a conditional inference problem: P(entailment | evidence, claim)
- Fine-tuned DeBERTa-based NLI models on domain-adapted QA corpora (10K+ examples)
- Evaluated calibration rigorously via precision–recall curves, Expected Calibration Error (ECE), and confidence distribution analysis across answer confidence bins
- Achieved ECE reduction from 0.23 → 0.07 through temperature scaling
- Identified systematic degradation under retrieval noise, semantic drift, and distribution shift
- Exposed failure modes including overconfident contradiction misclassification and hallucination in low-evidence contexts

---

#### 3. Multi-Agent RAG System for Decision Support (MedGuard)
*Agentic architecture for medical compliance*

- Architected a multi-agent decision pipeline with specialized agents for document ingestion, semantic chunking, regulatory reasoning, and audit report generation
- Integrated retrieval-augmented generation over regulatory corpora (CPT coding guidelines, CMS billing rules) using hybrid sparse-dense retrieval
- Employed re-ranking to enforce evidence-grounded outputs and reduce factual hallucination
- Analyzed trade-offs between latency, grounding fidelity, and response consistency under incomplete or contradictory inputs
- Systematically revealed failure modes of chain-of-thought reasoning under ambiguous regulatory constraints

---

#### 4. Causal Fairness Auditing in Predictive Models
*Counterfactual fairness framework*

- Designed a framework to audit ML models for direct and indirect discrimination using path-specific counterfactual effects
- Distinguishes legitimate from illegitimate causal pathways to sensitive attributes (e.g., gender, race)
- Implemented counterfactual fairness metrics and compared against statistical parity and equalized odds baselines
- Evaluated on tabular benchmark datasets (Adult Income, COMPAS)
- Revealed divergence between causal and statistical notions of fairness (up to 0.15 difference in metrics)
